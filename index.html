Lucard & CO presents

Lucard & Co, founded remotely a few hours ago by Richard Johnson, Davide Pacco and Luca Perullo, the company operates in the tourism industry according to the "integrated tour operator" formula, organizing trips and stays with destinations located in Sicily and Sardinia, and directly managing hotel facilities (all owned) which are proposed with an innovative and fully integrated offer (air transport, transfers, accommodation, recreational activities). At the head of a group of companies operating in the tourism sector, Travelly.io (company name) is the reference operator for the holidays organized in Sicily and Sardinia, destinations in which he strongly believed and invested, specializing day after day in the offer of weekly holidays in the club formula, guaranteeing excellent quality and maximum assistance at all times, and favoring the "family" segment with tariff concessions and targeted hotel services. One of the strengths is certainly the direct management of the clubs and resorts. A very important element that allows the company to constantly monitor the quality of the services offered (thanks to a control system that involves the customers themselves), and consequently to be able to intervene immediately, without any intermediation, to always keep the quality standard of the product high.
